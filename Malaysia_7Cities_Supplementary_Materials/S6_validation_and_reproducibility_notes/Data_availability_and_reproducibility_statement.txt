Data availability and reproducibility statement

The supplementary materials include the master dataset, data dictionary, original indicator workbooks, validation files, figure source data, and an analysis script. Figure 3, Figure 5, and Figure 6 can be reproduced from the CSV files in S5_figure_source_data_and_analysis_script. The script rerun_multidimensional_diagnosis.py reproduces the key z-score matrix, Ward clustering input, regime assignments, and regime profile table.

Recommended repository structure for submission:
- S1: Master dataset and variable dictionary.
- S2: TomTom FCD traffic-performance indicators.
- S3: OSM road-network indicators and recalibration validation.
- S4: Population-vehicle background proxy and raw selected parquet/CSV files.
- S5: Figure source data and reproducibility script.
- S6: Validation summaries and formula/method notes.

The TomTom indicators are treated as platform-based FCD benchmarks, not ground-truth traffic counts. Population and vehicle variables are state-level background proxies, not city-level demand counts. OSM indicators are interpreted as contextual road-network variables.
