The data and code supporting this study are available in Zenodo at DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.20032206. The archived package includes the final seven-city master dataset, TomTom-derived traffic-performance indicators, OpenStreetMap road-network indicators, OpenDOSM 2025 state-level population data extracts, data.gov.my JPJ 2025 vehicle-registration extracts, GIS boundary and road-edge files, Python scripts, validation reports, and documentation.

The final master dataset contains seven Malaysian cities and integrates four groups of variables: TomTom traffic-performance indicators, OpenStreetMap road-network structure indicators, OpenDOSM 2025 state-level population indicators, and data.gov.my JPJ 2025 vehicle-registration indicators. TomTom indicators are provided as derived city-level summary variables. OpenStreetMap data are attributed to OpenStreetMap contributors. Vehicle registration indicators are state-level registration-transaction proxies and should not be interpreted as city-level vehicle stock or active licensed vehicles.
